Migrations

Migrate Simple Planting Sites

This adds a stratum and a substratum to each planting site that has a site boundary but doesn't have strata or substrata, converting it to a detailed planting site. Planting sites that don't have site boundaries, or have boundaries of 0.05 hectares or less, aren't affected by this migration.

Recalculate Mortality Rates

This updates the stratum- and site-level mortality rates for a partial observation of a planting site to take into account plants from substrata that weren't observed this time.

Recalculate Planting Site Populations

This updates the reported plant counts for a planting site based on the total numbers of plants from the withdrawal log.
